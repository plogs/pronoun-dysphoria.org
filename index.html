What is pronoun dysphoria?

I'm an old school pronoun person.

My "identity" of who I am and what I should be is intimately tied to the use of traditional pronouns (e.g., he, she, them, they). I can't explain why; that's just who I am.

Because I am the way that I am, the use of so-called "preferred gender pronouns" feels disturbingly wrong. It causes anxiety. I feel bad inside. Some now call this condition pronoun dysphoria. dysphoria as in a profound state of unease or dissatisfaction.

Please respect who I am and refrain from inflicting harm by insisting or even implying that I should act in a way contrary to my innermost sense of self.
