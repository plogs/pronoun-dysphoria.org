What is pronoun dysphoria?

I'm an old-school-pronoun person.

My "identity" is tied to the use of traditional pronouns (e.g., he, she, them, they). I can't explain why; that's just who I am.

The use of so-called "preferred gender pronouns" feels disturbingly wrong. It causes anxiety. I feel bad inside. My condition is called pronoun dysphoria.

Please respect my point-of-view and refrain from inflicting harm on me by insisting or even implying that I should act in a way contrary to my innermost sense of self.
